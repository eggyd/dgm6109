Qing Dan — Lab 9: Best Two Scatterplots

My dataset was collected to explore the relationship between personality types and music listening habits, as well as how music influences people’s emotions and focus differently.
Each participant recorded their MBTI personality type, along with details such as listening duration (minutes per day), music genre, and self-reported scores of
Mood, Focus, and Stress (0–5 scale) after listening.
By analyzing these variables, I want to understand whether people with different personality traits (introverted vs. extroverted, thinking vs. feeling types, etc.)
show distinct patterns in their listening behavior, emotional responses, and levels of focus.
