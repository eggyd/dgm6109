Does Music Genre Affect Focus? (Lo-fi vs Hardstyle, Oct 17–20)

Different types of music influence people's emotional and cognitive states in different ways.
Relaxing genres such as Lo-fi tend to improve focus, while energetic genres such as Hardstyle lead to lower focus levels.

This visualization extends the original question by introducing mood level represented by bubble size.
It explores how different music genres influence not only focus but also emotional state.
when focus is high, does mood tend to drop?
And when the mood is elevated, could excitement make it harder to stay concentrated?

I chose this visualization because, like Plot 1, it maintains a clean and balanced layout, using the four-margin method in D3.js to achieve consistent spacing and alignment. I also implemented both sorting and filtering functions to refine the dataset, ensuring that the plotted relationships remain accurate and focused.

Beyond data structure, this chart integrates interactive hover effects and a continuous transition animation that makes the bubbles and legend gently pulse in color. This rhythmic motion was designed deliberately to echo the theme of music allowing feel more alive and conceptually connected to its subject.
